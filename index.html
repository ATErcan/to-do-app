TO-DO LIST

Welcome to My To-Do-List App. You can add your tasks to the list with the dates you have done or will do.
Your tasks will be added to the list. If you click on trash icon on your task, the task will moved to the do-it later section. But if you don't want to do that task anymore, go to the do-it later list and click on the trash icon again. This will remove your task compeletely. Is that all? No. If you check the checkbox of your task, the task will moved to the completed tasks section. And if you remove the tic, your task will moved to the do-it later section.
You can also make the tasks you added in the completed section not completed again, or even delete them in the same way.
